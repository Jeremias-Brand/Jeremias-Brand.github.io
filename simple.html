My Research Interests

Interest 1: Evolutionary Biology

My research explores the genetic and molecular basis of evolution, with a focus on how traits evolve across species.

Interest 2: Genomics and Bioinformatics

I analyze genomic data to uncover insights into evolutionary processes, utilizing advanced bioinformatics tools and pipelines.

Interest 3: Conservation Science

Understanding the impact of human activities on biodiversity and developing strategies to preserve endangered species are central to my work.
